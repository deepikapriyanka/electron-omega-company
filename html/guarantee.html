price match guarantee
  • We guarantee to match any competitor’s price by 10%
  • See terms and conditions for more details

Terms and conditions of price match guarantee
  • Electron Omega (known as “we” going forward) will match any competitor’s price by 10%.
  • For example, if Competitor ABC is offering web development services for a cost of $10,000, we will charge $9,000 for that same service.
  • The service needs to be the same as the competitor’s service.
  • For example, if Competitor ABC is offering a Microsoft Word training service for a cost of $1,000, we will offer that same Microsoft Word training service for a cost of $900.
  • Price matches will only be honored if we are given proof of the competitor’s price. For example, this proof can be in the form of a competitor’s online ad, newspaper ad, etc.
  • We will have sole discretion to determine the authenticity of this proof.
  • We have sole discretion to change the terms of this price match guarantee at any time.
  • Proof of a competitor’s price must be delivered before services have begun.
  • Price match guarantees will not be given retroactively. This means, for example, the price match guarantee will not be honored if proof of a competitor’s price is given to us after services have begun.
